Difficult Korean Word

What is your name? - 당신의 이름은 무엇인요? (dangsinui ireumeun mueosinyo?)
[dahng-shin-eui ee-reum-eun moo-eo-sing-ga-yo?]

What is dangsinui ireumeun mueosinyo?

This is a formal way to ask someone's name in Korean. It literally translates to "What is your name?" and is commonly used when meeting someone for the first time.

Used in a sentence

"bdangsinui ireumeun mueosinyo, Chingu?"
