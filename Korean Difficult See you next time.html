Difficult Korean Word

See you next time - 다음에 뵙겠습니다 (daeume boepgesseumnida)
[boh-eop-ge-sseum-ni-da]

What is daeume boepgesseumnida?

formal way of saying in Korean, usually used when parting from someone you expect to meet again in the future.

Used in a sentence

"Daeume boepgesseumnida, Francis"
