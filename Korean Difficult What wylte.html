Difficult Korean Word

What would you like to eat? - 뭐 드시고 싶어요? (mwo deusigo sipeoyo?)
[mwo deu-shi-go si-peo-yo?]

What is mwo deusigo sipeoyo?

is a polite way to ask someone what they would like to eat, typically when offering food or when ordering at a restaurant.

Used in a sentence

"Mwo deusigo sipeoyo, Jharell?"
